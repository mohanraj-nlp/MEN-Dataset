KUALA LUMPUR: Malaysia-born international film star Tan Sri Michelle Yeoh lent a helping hand on Christmas during clean-up after the recent flood in Taman Sri Muda, Shah Alam. The actress was also joined by Berjaya Group founder Tan Sri Vincent Tan. Both Yeoh and Tan took part in the post-flood cleanup with a team of the 502 Territorial Army under the 4th Malaysian Infantry Division (4 Div), Shah Alam City Council (MBSA) and the Taiwan Buddhist Tzu-Chi Malaysia Foundation. The clean-up took place at Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Sri Muda 2. Also present was Armed Forces chief General Tan Sri Affendi Buang. Yeoh after the clean-up said: 'It is truly heartwarming to see all here, regardless of religion and race. 'We are one. This is the true spirit of Christmas.'
